Black Space - Alana
=======================================

Background:
BlankSpace is a short story game about restoring light to the world shrouded in darkness.

=======================================

How to play:

1. Start by launching welcome.html from a browser.
2. Step through the story till the challenge locating torches.
3. On challenge completion, user has options to replay or go back to launch screen.

=======================================

Challenge: 

Users can use their arrow keys to collect the torches & recover the light. 
The number of torches is randomly generated by the function.
